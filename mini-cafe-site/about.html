Our Story

Founded in 2010 by two coffee-loving friends, Jenings Cafe started as a small cafe with a big dream: to bring exceptional coffee and a warm, inviting atmosphere to our community. Over the years, we've grown, but our commitment to quality and community remains at the heart of everything we do.
