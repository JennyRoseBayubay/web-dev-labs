Our Favorite K-pop Stars

BTS Jeon Jungkook

Jeon Jungkook is a South Korean singer-songwriter, dancer, rapper, producer and composer under Big Hit Entertainment. He is best known as the main vocalist, lead dancer, center, and maknae of BTS.

Read More

Born on September 1, 1997, in Busan, South Korea, Jungkook debuted with BTS in 2013. Known for his strong stage presence and versatile vocals, he has captivated fans worldwide. His solo songs like "Euphoria", "Seven", and "Still With You" are fan favorites. Jungkook is also known for his passion in art, fitness, and fashion.

BTS Park Jimin

Park Jimin is a South Korean singer-songwriter, dancer, and producer. He is the lead vocalist and one of the main dancers of BTS, known for his elegance and emotional depth.

Read More

Born on October 13, 1995, in Busan, Jimin studied contemporary dance before joining BTS. His solo songs like "Lie", "Serendipity", and "Like Crazy" highlight his expressive vocal tone and emotional performance style. Fans love his kindness and powerful presence on stage.

BTS Kim Taehyung

Kim Taehyung, known as V, is a South Korean singer, actor, and songwriter. He is recognized for his deep voice, strong visuals, and artistic flair.

Read More

Born on December 30, 1995, in Daegu, Taehyung is known for songs like "Stigma", "Singularity", and "Inner Child". He’s also acted in the drama Hwarang and is celebrated for his fashion sense, photography, and warm personality. V is considered one of K-pop’s most unique and soulful idols.
