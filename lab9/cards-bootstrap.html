About Bts Jeon Jungkook

Jeon Jungkook is a South Korean singer-songwriter, dancer, rapper, producer and composer under Big Hit Entertainment. He is best known as the main vocalist, lead dancer, center, and maknae of the worldwide sensational boy group BTS.

Read More

Born on September 1, 1997, in Busan, South Korea, Jungkook debuted with BTS in 2013. Known for his strong stage presence and versatile vocals, he has captivated fans worldwide. Outside BTS, he’s released solo tracks like "Euphoria," "Still With You," and "Seven" — showing his individual artistry. Jungkook also participates in songwriting, choreography, and production. His passion for art, fitness, and fashion has made him a Gen Z icon globally.

Bts Park Jimin

Park Jimin is a South Korean singer-songwriter, dancer, composer, and producer under Big Hit Entertainment HYBE Labels. He is best known as the lead vocalist and one of the main dancers of the worldwide sensation boy group BTS.

Read More

Jimin was born on October 13, 1995, in Busan, South Korea. He studied contemporary dance at Busan High School of Arts and later joined Big Hit. Known for his expressive and emotional performances, Jimin has released solo tracks like "Lie," "Serendipity," and "Like Crazy." His stage presence, powerful vocals, and graceful dance moves have earned him immense admiration from fans around the world.

Bts Kim Taehyung

Kim Taehyung, known under his stage name V, is a South Korean singer-songwriter, dancer, composer, producer, and actor under Big Hit Entertainment and HYBE Labels. He is best known as one of the vocalists and visuals of the worldwide sensation boy group BTS.

Read More

Born on December 30, 1995, in Daegu, South Korea, Taehyung is known for his deep, soulful voice and artistic charisma. He has released solo tracks like "Stigma," "Singularity," and "Inner Child." V is also a talented photographer and actor, having starred in the historical drama *Hwarang*. His fashion sense, warm personality, and unique artistry make him a standout star in and outside of BTS.
